Does PackPilot Pro support all thermal printers?

Yes, it works natively with all popular thermal printers including TSC, Xprinter, Zebra, and standard A6 size layout devices via USB/Network drivers.

Can I run this software on multiple office computers?

Yes, depending on your tier license, you can activate PackPilot desktop profiles across your entire worker network for streamlined catalog parcel picking.
