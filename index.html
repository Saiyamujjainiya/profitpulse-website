Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about managing your payout tracking safely.

Is my Meesho data completely secure?

Yes. ProfitPulse relies completely on secure cloud infrastructure patterns. We store data with advanced encryption profiles and never share or expose catalog pricing variables to external vendors.

How does it calculate Customer Return losses accurately?

We break down forward logistics penalties against return shipping costs automatically collected from your statement logs. This isolates pure manufacturing cost write-offs from simple shipping processing adjustments.

Can I upload custom sourcing costs for multiple SKUs?

Absolutely. You can update your operational product margins directly using a single-click CSV or Excel spreadsheet export. Your dashboard handles the cross-referencing calculations internally.
